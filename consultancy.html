Consultancy Areas

Our Areas of Expertise

Children and Youth Development

Our programs are designed to empower and support young individuals in their personal, educational, and social growth. They aim to help children and youths succeed in various aspects of their lives.

Women's Rights and Gender Equality

We guide organizations in designing, managing, and evaluating programs that integrate women’s rights and gender equality, conducting gender and social inclusion audits to reform policies and practices.

Economic Development and Sustainable Growth

Our services support organizations in integrating human rights-based approaches to sustainable development, focusing on human dignity, social and economic justice, and development programming.

Skills Enhancement Training for Key Program Staff

We provide comprehensive skills enhancement training tailored to key program staff, focusing on capacity building to enhance program delivery and impact.

Institutional Strategy Development and Leadership Training

Our consultancy includes strategic development services, financial management, and leadership training, equipping organizations with the skills for robust governance and operational excellence.

Other Consultancy Services
  • Team building and staff retreats
  • Board governance and management training
  • Program baselines/end line studies and evaluations
  • Organizational capacity assessments and strengthening
