What Are We Going to Approach?

At ATI, we are committed to developing your attitudes and skills for personal and societal leadership. You will approach state-of-the-art theories and research that will enable you to challenge the world’s governance and leadership systems.

What is Our Methodology?

We refer to our students as participants, fostering participatory and Competency-Based Education Training methodologies. In CBET training, greater attention is paid to assessing the quality of graduates from different institutions. Our methodologies emphasize the 3H: “Head, Hand, and Heart,” critical for transforming societies' ways of thinking, doing, and acting. You will therefore be challenged to participate in learning processes that will provide actionable insights.

Research

Producing research that will help shape the world. We are eager to understand “what is out there?” What can be said with evidence about Leadership, ECDE, Theology, and Community Development? We also want to know “Why?” – Why is the situation as it is? This evidence opens the (applied science) question: “What can be done about it?” Neither theory nor research can tell us what to do, but good research can provide us with evidence for informed decisions for change.
