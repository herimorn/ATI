About Us

Welcome to ATI

More than 10 years of academic, professional and capacity development for society transformation.

ATI is a training center situated in Arusha, Tanzania, dedicated to high-quality academic and capacity development for social transformation, especially for religious leaders aiming for global change. With our motto, "we open the world," we have empowered leaders, youth, and communities through training in leadership, theology, community development, international languages, hospitality services, and vocational skills.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality capacity development that fosters social transformation, ensuring that those we serve are equipped to drive social change and open their world Acts 17.6

Our Goal

We aim to inspire as many people as possible, collaborating with Churches, organizations, agencies, and networks globally to achieve impactful results in societal transformation through Education.
